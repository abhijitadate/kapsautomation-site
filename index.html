Our Excellence

Key Achievements

Discover our proven track record of delivering innovative automation solutions across diverse industries

Pharmaceuticals
ISA S88 Standards Implementation

Performed comprehensive front-end analysis to architect robust solutions per ISA S88 standards. Successfully implemented and integrated automation solutions for large-scale bulk drug substance manufacturing facilities, ensuring regulatory compliance and operational excellence.

Process Automation
Lab-Scale Pilot Plant Automation

Architected and implemented comprehensive automation solutions for lab-scale pilot plants featuring continuous processes. Integrated PI historian, PAT (Process Analytical Technology), and centralized batch reporting systems for enhanced data visibility and control.

Manufacturing Excellence
Paperless CGMP Manufacturing

Designed, architected, and implemented cutting-edge automation solutions for continuous CGMP manufacturing facilities. Achieved the ambitious goal of establishing paperless manufacturing processes, significantly reducing documentation errors and improving traceability.

Life Sciences
Vaccine Facility Commissioning

Architected and implemented automation solutions for vaccine facilities from startup through commissioning. Successfully executed comprehensive I/O and OQ (Installation Qualification and Operational Qualification) protocols, ensuring full regulatory compliance and operational readiness.
